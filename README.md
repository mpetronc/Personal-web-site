# Personal Website

A separate creative landing page that can also work as a lightweight CV.

## Files

- `index.html`
- `styles.css`
- `script.js`

## Open locally

You can open `index.html` directly in a browser, or serve the folder with any simple local server.

## What to customize

- your bio text
- your real email and profile links
- your school / work / internship details
- your projects
